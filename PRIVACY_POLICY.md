# Privacy Policy for Arium

**Last Updated:** December 2024

## Introduction

Arium ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Habit Data**: Titles, notes, completion dates, and streaks for your habits
- **User Preferences**: Theme selections, language preferences, notification settings
- **Premium Status**: Purchase information (handled by Apple, we only receive confirmation)

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, iOS version (for crash reporting and compatibility)
- **Usage Data**: App launch times, feature usage (anonymous, for improving the app)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the Arium app functionality
- Sync your data across your devices (if you enable iCloud)
- Send you notifications and reminders (if you enable them)
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Process premium purchases through Apple's In-App Purchase system

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All your habit data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage
- Data is encrypted using iOS built-in encryption

### iCloud Storage (Optional)
- If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account
- We use Apple's CloudKit framework with end-to-end encryption
- Only you can access your iCloud data through your Apple ID

### We Do NOT:
- Store your data on our servers
- Share your data with third parties
- Sell your personal information
- Track your location
- Access your contacts or photos

## Third-Party Services

### Apple Services
- **StoreKit**: For processing premium purchases (Apple's privacy policy applies)
- **CloudKit**: For optional iCloud sync (Apple's privacy policy applies)
- **HealthKit**: For optional health data integration (requires explicit permission)

### No Other Third Parties
We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all your data within the app
- **Export**: Export your data in JSON or CSV format (Settings → Export Data)
- **Delete**: Delete all your data (Settings → Clear All Habits)
- **Opt-Out**: Disable iCloud sync at any time (Settings → iCloud Sync)

## Children's Privacy

Arium is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Retained until you delete the app or clear data manually
- **iCloud Data**: Retained in your iCloud account until you delete it
- **Purchase History**: Retained by Apple according to their policies

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Showing an in-app notification for significant changes

## GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:

### Legal Basis for Processing
We process your data based on:
- **Consent**: For optional features like iCloud sync and notifications
- **Contract**: To provide the app services you requested
- **Legitimate Interest**: To improve the app and fix bugs

### Your GDPR Rights
- Right to access your personal data
- Right to rectification of inaccurate data
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@ariumapp.com

### Data Protection Officer
For GDPR-related inquiries: dpo@ariumapp.com

## CCPA Compliance (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

### Your CCPA Rights
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to say no to the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
- Right to access your personal information
- Right to delete your personal information
- Right to equal service and price

**We do not sell your personal information.**

To exercise your CCPA rights, contact us at: privacy@ariumapp.com
